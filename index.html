History

Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI) was established in 1887 and was formerly known as Victoria Jubilee Technical Institute. The institute is reputed for excellent training in courses in engineering and technology at diploma and, degree and post graduate levels over three thousand students. Over twenty seven courses are offered at the undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, postgraduate diploma and post diploma levels. The institute has been granted financial and academic autonomy from June 21, 2004. The institute is affiliated to the University of Mumbai and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Government of India. The institute is also recognized as a center for research. The institute has been awarded ‘A’ grade by the Government of Maharashtra (India). The institute has been selected for TEQIP Phase II assistance by the World Bank.
